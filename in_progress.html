• NIRO Educational Bot

  • Research Project — supported by NSU Intelligent Robotics (NIRO) Lab

  • October 2024 — Present

  • Overview: At the NSU Intelligent Robotics Lab, we are developing the NIRO EDUCATIONAL Bot to support research and hands-on learning. It includes a gyroscope, IR sensors, motors, WiFi, and uses Raspberry Pi with Arduino for processing. The 3D-printed, PCB-based design with a differential drive system allows easy integration of LiDAR, depth cameras, and other hardware.

  • Project Hex

  • Research Project — supported by NSU Intelligent Robotics (NIRO) Lab

  • February 2025 — Present

  • Overview: Wheeled robots perform well on flat surfaces but struggle on rough terrain. Hexapod robots, with six 3-DOF legs, provide better stability and adaptability. Our hexapod uses 18 high-torque servos, an Arduino controller, and a LiPo battery, with modular support for components like Raspberry Pi, Lidar, and depth sensors for enhanced autonomy and mapping.

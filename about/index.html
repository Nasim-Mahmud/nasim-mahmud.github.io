Overview

He is a researcher passionate about building intelligent machines that work together to solve complex problems. His projects span from disaster response robots to assistive technologies, combining innovation, teamwork, and a drive to create systems that adapt to real-world challenges. Currently, he is researching swarm robotics with a focus on both centralized and decentralized control systems.


In his personal life, he enjoys playing football and collecting coins, with a particular fondness for the colors cyan and teal. During leisure time, he engages in deep discussions about games with his father. Although he does not have a favorite flower, he finds butterflies unpredictable and frightening, yet is fascinated by the concept of "The butterfly effect", this fascination inspired him to write a short story about it a few years ago.
